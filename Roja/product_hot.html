Habanero

Melinda's

Heat 9/10

Melinda's blends fresh carrots, onions, garlic, and a hint of lime juice with the Habanero. The result is a pepper sauce that harmonizes heat and flavor without the overpowering pungency found in traditional vinegar-based hot sauces. Melinda's vegetable base allows you to spice your food without drowning out the original flavor.

Made in Colombia.
