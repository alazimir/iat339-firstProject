Our History

1832

Our founder Fernando de Rojas found Roja Hotsauce with the sole purpose of melting your taste buds. After getting injured in the battle of Salamanca in 1812 Fernando was relieved from his duty in the army. He quickly became bored and began experimenting with varying peppers Portugal in an effort to capture the intense experience of his time in the war.

1851

After experimenting for several years our founder decided to begin branding Roja as the hot sauce of the future. He grasped the attention of The Governor of Salamanca which led him to try out Roja’s hot sauce. The first sign of Roja become an established brand around Portugal becomes a reality!

1872

Our great founder Fernando de Rojas passes away at 82. He left the legacy that is now known globally as Roja hot sauce!

1920

Roja hot sauce becomes an established brand for Portugal, some even begin suggesting that it gets added to the national flag but the government votes it down. Regardless, progress continues and the brand begins to get recognition in some European countries.

1992

Roja hot sauce.com goes live after the 1-year anniversary of the Internet being released to the public!

2008

Roja finally arrives in America and begins to compete with Frank’s red hot to capture the top spot for hot sauce around the world! At the same time, their online shop quickly becomes one of the top hot sauce marketplaces.

2020

The brand is still strong in 2020 with veery exciting news for the future! We plan on expanding globally and we hope to get a partnership with SpaceX and become the first brand to serve the people of Mars in the future! We have been having on going discussion with Elon Musk and we will keep you updated!
